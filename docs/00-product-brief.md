# Product Brief

## Product Name

Public project name: Design Partner Insight Engine.

Working codename: SignalForge.

## One-Liner

SignalForge turns messy design-partner notes into structured product discovery insights, evidence-backed pilot criteria, and recommended next experiments.

## Target Demo Story

A founder or venture studio lead has scattered notes from early design partners. They create a project, paste synthetic notes, run extraction, review the evidence, and leave with a clearer view of what to build now, what to learn more about, and what to ignore.

## Positioning

SignalForge is a lightweight product discovery workspace for early venture teams. It is not a research repository or a general note-taking tool. It is a focused demo of how AI can organize ambiguous qualitative signal while preserving human judgment.

## Primary Value

- Compress messy discovery input into usable product signal.
- Preserve evidence so recommendations are explainable.
- Help teams choose the next experiment instead of creating a long feature wish list.
- Demonstrate full-stack TypeScript, MongoDB/Mongoose modeling, and AI provider abstraction in a practical artifact.

## Design Principles

- Evidence before recommendation.
- Human review before acceptance.
- Simple flows over broad platform scope.
- Synthetic data by default.
- Product discovery language throughout.
- Small implementation slices that remain demoable.

## What Good Looks Like

- A reviewer can quickly see the strongest signals.
- Every accepted insight has a source note or evidence snippet.
- Decision recommendations are clear: build now, learn more, or ignore.
- The demo can be explained in 30 seconds and explored in 3 minutes.
- The codebase stays distinct from unrelated product domains.

## Demo Nature

SignalForge is intentionally synthetic/demo-first. It should not contain real customer notes, private company information, recruiter conversations, secrets, API keys, or credentials.
