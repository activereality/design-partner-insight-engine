# Gitwit Talking Points

## Product-Minded Engineering

SignalForge starts from a real product question: how should a venture team turn messy discovery notes into decisions? The implementation plan follows the workflow instead of leading with infrastructure.

## Zero-To-One Venture Building

The artifact is scoped like an early venture experiment. It defines a tight user, a narrow product loop, a synthetic demo scenario, and a path from ambiguous notes to next experiments.

## Design Partner Workflow

The core workflow maps to how design-partner learning actually happens: collect notes, identify patterns, preserve evidence, review assumptions, and choose the next test.

## AI Product Design

AI is used as a structured extraction assistant, not an authority. The product keeps evidence attached and requires human review before insights become trusted.

## Full-Stack TypeScript

The target stack uses React + TypeScript and NestJS + TypeScript so product contracts can stay consistent across frontend and backend.

## MongoDB/Mongoose Decision

MongoDB/Mongoose fits evolving discovery data: notes, evidence snippets, draft insights, review states, and quality flags. It supports practical iteration during the MVP.

## Human-In-The-Loop Judgment

The review workflow is the product. Accepting, editing, rejecting, and marking follow-up are what turn AI output into product judgment.

## End-To-End Ownership

SignalForge shows product framing, architecture, API design, frontend workflow, AI abstraction, data modeling, demo planning, and implementation sequencing.

## Conflict-Safe Boundary

SignalForge is separate from PlantBrain/Tenzin because it focuses only on startup/product discovery and design-partner insight synthesis, not industrial maintenance, equipment troubleshooting, machinery, manuals, technician copilots, CMMS, diagnostics, or field service.

## Concise Interview Phrases

- "I wanted the artifact to show product judgment, not just scaffolding."
- "The AI output is useful only when the evidence stays attached."
- "Mock provider first keeps the demo reliable and testable."
- "MongoDB fits the document-shaped nature of discovery notes and extracted insights."
- "The key workflow is human review of AI-assisted synthesis."
